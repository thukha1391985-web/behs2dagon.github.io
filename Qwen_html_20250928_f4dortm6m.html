About BEHS 2 Dagon Myoma

Basic Education High School No. 2 Dagon (BEHS 2 Dagon Myoma) is one of the premier educational institutions in Yangon, Myanmar. Our school is committed to providing quality education that nurtures academic excellence, character development, and civic responsibility.

We follow the national curriculum while emphasizing critical thinking, creativity, and practical skills that prepare our students for higher education and future careers.

Our History

BEHS 2 Dagon Myoma has a rich heritage dating back to 1954. Originally established as Dagon Myoma National High School, our school has grown from humble beginnings into one of the most respected educational institutions in the Dagon Township.

Over the decades, we have produced countless graduates who have gone on to become leaders in various fields including medicine, engineering, education, business, and public service. Our alumni network spans across Myanmar and internationally, maintaining the proud traditions and values instilled during their time at our school.

The school has consistently maintained high academic standards and has been recognized for its contributions to education in Myanmar.

Why BEHS 2 Dagon Myoma is the Best Choice

Academic Excellence: Our students consistently achieve outstanding results in national examinations, with many securing top positions in university entrance rankings.

Experienced Faculty: Our teaching staff comprises highly qualified and dedicated educators with years of experience in their respective subjects.

Comprehensive Curriculum: Beyond academics, we offer a well-rounded education including sports, arts, music, and extracurricular activities that develop the whole student.

Strong Values: We emphasize moral education, discipline, respect, and community service, preparing students to be responsible citizens.

Modern Facilities: Our school is equipped with well-equipped science laboratories, a comprehensive library, computer lab, sports field, and modern classrooms.

Alumni Success: Our graduates are well-prepared for university and have a strong track record of success in their chosen careers.
